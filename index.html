🎯 My Product Philosophy
01

Outcomes over output. I don't measure success by features shipped — I measure it by behaviour changed, revenue moved, or cost eliminated. Every roadmap item must answer "so what?"

02

Ambiguity is the job. Senior PMs aren't hired to execute clear briefs — they're hired to create clarity where none exists. I lean into fuzzy problems and build the structure others can execute against.

03

Build to understand. The fastest way to validate an assumption is a working prototype, not a slide deck. I prototype to learn, then scale what works — keeping engineering effort focused on proven bets.
